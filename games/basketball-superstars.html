Basketball Superstars

Game Description

Basketball Superstars puts you in the shoes of an aspiring basketball champion. Create your player, master various skills, and compete against other players in intense matches. With realistic gameplay mechanics and stunning graphics, you'll experience the thrill of professional basketball right from your browser.

Controls

  • WASD / Arrow Keys - Move player
  • Space - Jump / Shoot
  • E - Pass ball
  • Q - Steal / Block
  • Tab - View scoreboard
