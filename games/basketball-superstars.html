Game Overview

Basketball Superstars brings the excitement of professional basketball to your screen with realistic gameplay mechanics and special character abilities. Choose from a roster of unique players, each with their own strengths and signature moves, as you compete in tournaments and exhibition matches to become the ultimate basketball champion.

• Multiple game modes including Tournament and Exhibition
• 12 unique superstar characters with special abilities
• Realistic basketball physics and animations

How to Play

  1. Select your game mode from the main menu (Tournament, Exhibition, or Practice)
  2. Choose your superstar character based on their unique strengths and abilities
  3. Use the arrow keys to move your player around the court
  4. Press A to shoot when you have the ball, or to attempt a steal on defense
  5. Press S to pass to a teammate or D to activate your character's special ability
  6. Score more points than your opponent before time runs out to win the game
