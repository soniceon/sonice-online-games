Game Overview

Spider Solitaire is a challenging variant of the classic solitaire card game that uses two decks of cards. Sort cards in descending order to create complete sequences and clear the board. With multiple difficulty levels, this game offers a great challenge for both beginners and expert players.

• Multiple difficulty levels (1, 2, or 4 suits)
• Realistic card animations and intuitive controls
• Unlimited undo and strategic hint system

How to Play

  1. Create descending sequences of cards (King to Ace) of the same suit.
  2. You can move a card or a sequence of cards to another pile if they form a valid descending sequence.
  3. Complete a sequence from King to Ace to automatically remove it from the table.
  4. When you run out of moves, click the stock pile to deal a new row of cards.
