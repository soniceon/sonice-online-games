Game Overview

Solitaire TriPeaks Garden combines the classic TriPeaks solitaire gameplay with beautiful garden-themed backgrounds and relaxing music. In this card game, you'll work to clear all cards from the pyramid-like layout by selecting cards that are one rank higher or lower than the card shown on the waste pile, regardless of suit. As you progress through levels, you'll unlock new garden environments with increasing challenges.

• Beautiful garden-themed backgrounds
• Multiple levels with increasing difficulty
• Relaxing gameplay with soothing music

How to Play

  1. The goal is to remove all cards from the three pyramid peaks
  2. You can select any exposed card that is one rank higher or lower than the card on the waste pile (the card shown at the bottom)
  3. For example, if the waste pile shows a 7, you can select either a 6 or an 8
  4. Kings can be matched with Aces (and vice versa) to create a continuous sequence
  5. Try to create long sequences of cards to earn combo bonuses and higher scores
  6. If you get stuck, you can use the hint button or draw a new card from the stock pile
  7. Be strategic with your moves, as some cards might block access to other cards you need
  8. Clear all cards from the board to complete each level and progress through the garden
