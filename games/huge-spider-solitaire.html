Game Overview

Huge Spider Solitaire is an expanded version of the classic Spider Solitaire card game. The goal is to arrange all cards in descending order by suit (from King to Ace) to create complete sequences that are automatically removed from the table. The game offers multiple difficulty levels - play with one suit for beginners, two suits for intermediate players, or the challenging four-suit mode for experts. Strategic planning and careful moves are essential to victory!

• Three difficulty levels: One, Two, or Four suits
• Multiple undo options and hint system
• Score tracking with time-based bonuses

How to Play

  1. The goal is to arrange all cards in descending sequences by suit (King to Ace)
  2. You can move cards between columns if they form a descending sequence (regardless of suit)
  3. For example, you can place a 9 of any suit on a 10 of any suit
  4. However, you can only move a sequence of cards together if they are all of the same suit
  5. Empty columns can be filled with any card or sequence
  6. When you form a complete sequence of 13 cards of the same suit (King to Ace), it will automatically be removed
  7. Click the deck at the bottom to deal new cards when you run out of moves
  8. Each deal distributes one card to each of the ten columns
  9. You must clear all cards by forming complete sequences to win the game
  10. For easier gameplay, start with one-suit mode, then progress to two and four suits as you gain experience
