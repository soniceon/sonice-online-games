About Space Shooter

Space Shooter is an action-packed arcade game where your mission is to defend Earth from waves of alien invaders. Control your spaceship, dodge enemy fire, and blast the aliens before they reach our planet.

As you progress through the levels, you'll face increasingly difficult enemies and boss battles. Collect power-ups to upgrade your weapons and shields, allowing you to take on even the most formidable alien forces.

The game features colorful retro-inspired graphics, intense gameplay, and an exciting soundtrack that will keep you engaged for hours. Can you achieve the highest score and become Earth's ultimate defender?

Game Controls

Movement

Use Arrow Keys or W A S D to move your spaceship.

Mobile users can use the on-screen joystick for movement.

Shooting

Press Space or Left Mouse Button to fire your weapons.

Mobile users can tap the fire button on the right side of the screen.

Special Ability

Press E or Right Mouse Button to activate your special ability when charged.

Pause

Press P or ESC to pause the game.

Strategy Tips

1

Prioritize Targets

Focus on destroying the most dangerous enemies first. Enemies with missile launchers or laser beams can quickly end your game if left unchecked.

2

Collect Power-ups

Don't miss power-ups dropped by defeated enemies. These upgrades can significantly improve your firepower and survival chances.

3

Keep Moving

A stationary target is an easy target. Always keep moving to avoid enemy fire and stay unpredictable.
