Game Overview

Snow Rider 3D puts you on a snowboard for an exhilarating downhill adventure across beautiful winter landscapes. Navigate through challenging terrain, perform tricks to earn points, and race against the clock to set new records. With smooth 3D graphics and responsive controls, you'll experience the thrill of snowboarding from the comfort of your browser.

• Multiple challenging tracks with varied terrain
• Trick system for earning bonus points
• Realistic 3D graphics and physics

How to Play

  1. Use the left and right arrow keys (or A and D) to steer your snowboarder down the slope
  2. Press the up arrow key (or W) to increase your speed and down arrow (or S) to slow down
  3. Hit the space bar to jump when approaching ramps or obstacles
  4. While in the air, perform tricks by pressing different directional keys
  5. Land properly to avoid crashes (make sure your board is aligned with the slope)
  6. Collect coins and power-ups along the way to increase your score
  7. Complete tracks as quickly as possible to earn time bonuses
  8. Unlock new tracks and boards as you progress through the game
