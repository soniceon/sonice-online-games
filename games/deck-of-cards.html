Game Overview

Deck of Cards Mahjong combines the strategic gameplay of Mahjong with the familiar symbols and patterns of traditional playing cards. Your goal is to match and remove pairs of identical cards from the board. A card is available for matching only when its left or right edge is free (not blocked by other cards). Think carefully about each move, as the order in which you remove cards affects your ability to clear the board!

• Multiple challenging layouts and difficulty levels
• Beautiful card designs and smooth animations
• Time-based scoring system with bonus points

How to Play

  1. The goal is to clear all cards from the board by matching pairs
  2. Two cards can be matched if they have identical ranks and suits (e.g., both are King of Hearts)
  3. You can only select cards that are "free" - meaning at least one side (left or right) is not blocked by another card
  4. Click on one card, then click on its matching pair to remove both from the board
  5. Plan your moves carefully - removing cards in the wrong order can leave you with unmatchable pairs
  6. If you get stuck, you can use the hint feature to highlight available matches
  7. The shuffle button can help rearrange cards when no more moves are possible
  8. Clear the board as quickly as possible for the highest score and time bonuses
  9. Complete all levels to win the game and unlock new challenges
