Game Description

Jump into the action with Crazy Shooters! Experience intense multiplayer battles, unlock powerful weapons, and compete against players worldwide. With fast-paced gameplay and various game modes, every match brings new challenges and excitement.

Controls

  • WASD / Arrow Keys - Move character
  • Left Mouse - Shoot
  • R - Reload weapon
  • Space - Jump
  • 1-9 - Switch weapons
  • Tab - Show scoreboard
