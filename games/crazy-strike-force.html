Crazy Strike Force

Game Description

Experience intense shooting action in Crazy Strike Force! Battle through waves of enemies, upgrade your weapons, and defend your position in this action-packed game. With multiple weapons to choose from and increasingly challenging levels, your skills will be put to the test.

Controls

  • WASD - Move character
  • Mouse - Aim and shoot
  • R - Reload weapon
  • Space - Jump
  • 1-4 - Switch weapons
