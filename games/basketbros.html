BasketBros

Game Description

BasketBros brings multiplayer basketball mayhem to your screen! Choose your unique character, each with special abilities, and compete in fast-paced matches against players worldwide. Master different shot types, perform crazy dunks, and dominate the court in this action-packed basketball game.

Controls

  • WASD / Arrow Keys - Move character
  • Spacebar - Jump
  • Left Mouse - Shoot / Block
  • Right Mouse - Special ability
  • E - Emote
