Game Overview

BasketBros is a wacky basketball game that turns the traditional sport on its head with physics-based gameplay and chaotic action. Play against friends on the same keyboard or challenge the computer in this hilarious take on basketball where skill meets luck and anything can happen!

• Local multiplayer for up to 4 players
• Ragdoll physics for unpredictable fun
• Multiple game modes and courts

How to Play

  1. Select a game mode (1-on-1, 2-on-2, etc.) from the main menu
  2. Choose your character and team colors
  3. Use the movement keys to run and jump around the court
  4. Press the action button to shoot when you have the ball or steal/block when on defense
  5. Score more points than your opponent before time runs out
