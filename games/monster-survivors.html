About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where your objective is to stay alive as long as possible while facing increasingly difficult waves of monsters. Collect experience points to level up and unlock powerful abilities.

With each level, you'll be able to choose from a variety of upgrades to customize your character's abilities and adapt to different enemy types. Strategic planning and quick reflexes are essential to survive the monster apocalypse!

The game features vibrant graphics, dynamic gameplay, and an engaging progression system that will keep you coming back for more. Can you set a new survival record?

  • ✓ RPG-style level progression
  • ✓ Multiple character abilities
  • ✓ Dynamic difficulty scaling
  • ✓ Exciting power-ups and items

Game Controls

Movement

Use W A S D keys or arrow keys to move your character.

Mobile users can use the on-screen joystick for movement.

Abilities

Press Space to use your primary ability.

Mobile users can tap the ability button on the right side of the screen.

Level Up

When you gain enough experience to level up, a selection of upgrades will appear. Click or tap on your desired upgrade to select it.

Special Items

Collect special items dropped by defeated monsters for temporary boosts and power-ups.

Strategy Tips

1

Keep Moving

Standing still is a death sentence. Always keep moving to avoid being surrounded by monsters.

2

Prioritize Upgrades

Choose upgrades that complement your playstyle. Balance between offensive abilities, movement speed, and area-of-effect attacks.

3

Collect Experience

Don't forget to collect experience gems dropped by defeated monsters. These are crucial for leveling up and gaining new abilities.
