Game Overview

Tanuki Sunset is a stylish longboarding game with a synthwave aesthetic where you play as a raccoon skating down colorful, winding roads. Experience the thrill of downhill racing while performing tricks, collecting coins, and avoiding obstacles. The game features a relaxing atmosphere combined with challenging gameplay and a killer synthwave soundtrack.

• Multiple unique tracks with vibrant environments
• Customizable character and longboard options
• Relaxing synthwave soundtrack to cruise to

How to Play

  1. Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your raccoon on the longboard
  2. Maintain balance while going downhill at high speeds
  3. Collect coins along the track to unlock new customization options
  4. Hold Space to drift around tight corners
  5. Press Shift while in the air to perform tricks for extra points
  6. Avoid obstacles and falling off the track
  7. Complete tracks to unlock new levels and challenges
