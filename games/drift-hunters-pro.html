Drift Hunters Pro

Game Description

Drift Hunters Pro takes racing to the next level with realistic physics and stunning graphics. Choose from a variety of high-performance cars, customize them to your liking, and master the art of drifting across multiple tracks. Earn points, unlock new vehicles, and become the ultimate drift king!

Controls

  • WASD / Arrow Keys - Drive car
  • Space - Handbrake
  • Shift - Manual gear shift
  • C - Change camera view
  • R - Reset car position
