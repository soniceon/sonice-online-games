About Drift King

Experience the ultimate drifting sensation with Drift King, the most thrilling online racing game that puts your drifting skills to the test. Feel the adrenaline rush as you slide your car through sharp turns and challenging tracks, leaving a trail of smoke behind.

In Drift King, precision and style are equally important. Master the perfect balance between speed and control as you navigate through diverse environments ranging from urban streets to mountain passes. Earn points for perfect drifts, near misses, and stylish maneuvers that will help you climb the global leaderboards.

The game features stunning 3D graphics with realistic physics and smoke effects that will make you feel like a professional drift racer. With multiple cars to choose from and various tracks to master, Drift King offers endless hours of entertainment.

Game Controls

Keyboard Controls

Use Arrow Keys or W A S D to control your car's movement.

Press Space to activate the handbrake for drifting.

Use Shift for nitro boost.

Press R to reset your car if you get stuck.

Press ESC to pause the game.

Mobile Controls

Use the on-screen steering wheel or tilt your device to steer.

Tap the acceleration button on the right side to speed up.

Tap the brake button to slow down or reverse.

Use the dedicated drift button to initiate drifts.
